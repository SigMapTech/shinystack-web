Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2024

ShinyStack ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

ShinyStack is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

  • Local Data Only: All your precious metals collection data (items, photos, values) is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data.
  • No Account Required: You don't need to create an account or provide personal information to use ShinyStack.
  • No Cloud Sync: Your data is not uploaded to any cloud servers.

Data Storage

All data you enter into ShinyStack remains on your device. This includes:

  • Your precious metals inventory
  • Photos of your items
  • Purchase information and notes
  • App preferences and settings

Third-Party Services

ShinyStack may connect to third-party services for the following purposes:

  • Spot Price Data: We may fetch current precious metals prices from market data providers. These requests do not include any of your personal or collection data.

Data Security

Since your data stays on your device, its security depends on your device's security. We recommend:

  • Using a strong device passcode
  • Enabling device encryption
  • Keeping your device's operating system updated

Your Rights

Since we don't collect or store your data, you have complete control over your information. You can:

  • Delete all app data by uninstalling the app
  • Export your data using in-app features
  • Manage app permissions in your device settings

Children's Privacy

ShinyStack is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date.
